Jefferson Manor Park

Fairfax County · 14 acres
📍 ~0.5 mi · 2909 Farmington Dr

The neighborhood's go-to park for dog walking and, come winter, sledding. The gentle hill is a local favorite when snow hits. Also features a baseball diamond, basketball court, playground, picnic areas, and an ADA-accessible trail.

Huntley Meadows Park

Fairfax County · 1,500 acres
📍 ~3 mi · 3701 Lockheed Blvd

Fairfax County's largest park and one of the biggest freshwater wetlands in the DC metro area. A half-mile accessible boardwalk winds through a 500-acre marsh where you can spot great blue herons, egrets, beavers, turtles, and over 200 bird species. Stunning in every season — wildflowers in spring, vivid fall foliage, and golden marsh grasses in winter. Visitor center, observation towers, and 3.8 miles of trails. Free admission. Dogs allowed on woodland trails only.

Greendale Golf Course

Public · 18 Holes · Par 70
📍 ~2 mi · 6700 Telegraph Rd

A public 18-hole regulation course on 148 acres of rolling terrain right off Telegraph Road. Tight Bermuda fairways, water hazards, and a spectacular 17th-hole view of the entire course. Features a clubhouse with food service, pro shop, putting green, driving range, cart rentals, and private lessons. Open year-round.

Franconia Recreation Center

Fairfax County
📍 ~2 mi · 6601 Telegraph Rd⏰ Mon–Fri 5am–9pm · Sat 7am–6pm · Sun 8am–6pm

The beloved "Chessie's Big Backyard" playground and "Our Special Harbor" spray park make it a summer staple for families. Summer swim camps and classes for all ages run throughout the season.
